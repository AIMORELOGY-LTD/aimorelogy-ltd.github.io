# AIMORELOGY ? Key Facts

## Company & Focus
- AIMORELOGY builds FPV/UAV flight controllers, edge AI vision chips, AI tracking, and adaptive DShot control for drones and gimbal systems. We provide customized SOLUTION services and system integration. AIMORELOGY is a partner of SOPHGO, RT-Thread, and RISC-V.
- AIMORELOGY develops FPV/UAV flight controllers, edge AI vision chips, and tracking technology for drones and gimbal systems.

## Core Technologies
- Low-latency visual tracking for FPV/UAV missions, gimbal stabilization, and autonomous target following.
- Adaptive DShot control protocol for FPV/UAV ESCs with stable bidirectional motor control and DMA-free implementation.
- Cloud AI camera stack combining edge AI vision with scalable cloud orchestration and monitoring.

## Flight Controllers
- AIMORELOGY AFC-V1 is an all-in-one FPV/UAV flight controller integrating vision, video link, and control.

## Chips & Core Components
- SOPHGO Vision/TPU
  - BM1688: SOPHGO BM1688 edge TPU SoC combines an 8-core ARM CPU and 16 TOPS (32 TOPS INT4) with 8K imaging and multi-sensor ISP for drone/FPV vision, gimbal tracking, AI NVRs, robotics, and edge servers. Datasheet
  - CV186x: SOPHGO CV186x/CV186AH delivers 7.2 TOPS AI compute, binocular depth DPU, and 8MP@75fps encoding for multi-lens IPC, drones/FPV, gimbal vision, and robotics platforms. Datasheet
  - CV184x: SOPHGO CV184x series (CV1841C, CV1842C-P, CV1842H-P/CV1842HP, CV1843H-P) offers 1.0-1.5 TOPS TPU, ISP V4.0, and Intra4 encoding for FPV/UAV vision, gimbal tracking, smart cameras, and access control. Datasheet
  - CV181x: SOPHGO CV181x series (CV1813H, CV1812H, CV1811H, CV1812C-P, CV1811C, CV1810C, CV1813H-A, CV1812H-A, CV1811H-A, CV1812C-PA, CV1811C-A) delivers 0.5-1.0 TOPS AI for smart doorbells, access control, lightweight FPV/UAV cameras, and low-power AIoT. Datasheet
  - CV180x: SOPHGO CV180x series (CV1801B, CV180ZB) is a cost-effective 0.2 TOPS RISC-V vision SoC for entry FPV/UAV cameras, battery devices, and lightweight AIoT. Datasheet
- STM32 ARM Cortex-M
  - STM32F405: STM32F405xx Arm Cortex-M4 MCU with FPU, DSP, 168 MHz performance, 1 MB Flash, and rich connectivity for real-time control applications. Datasheet
  - STM32F722: STM32F722xx Arm Cortex-M7 MCU with single-precision FPU, DSP, 216 MHz performance, 512 KB Flash, and dual-mode QSPI. Datasheet
  - STM32F767: STM32F767xx High-end Arm Cortex-M7 MCU with double-precision FPU, up to 2 MB Flash, 512 KB SRAM, Ethernet, and Chrom-ART graphics. Datasheet

  - ESP32-S3-WROOM-1: ESP32-S3-WROOM-1 is a dual-core Wi-Fi + Bluetooth LE module with AI acceleration for smart home, voice, and edge AI devices. Datasheet
